About

The aging of our beloved ones faces us to the laws of life: taking affectively of them. But this reality faces us that most of the time we spend it in routine and repetitive work and the quality time that we want to give them was replaced with cumbersome procedures, reminders, visits

With this in our minds, we figured out a need to develop a device that can take care of this tasks which were a big load for people who takes care of elder persons, in special women.

That’s how arquitas was created.

This device will stop the necessity of human care once and for all. Would make more much easier the relation between families, elder people and caregivers. Besides, the community that forms among users of Arquitas greatly stimulates the reason for living of these people. At the same time, it will save both families and medical institutions a lot of resources. And you'll reduce your environmental impact and carbon footprint by drastically reducing travel.

That’s why we are convinced that a better life is possible.
